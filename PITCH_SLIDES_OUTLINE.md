# Elite Trader Pitch Slides Outline

## 1. Cover

- Title: Elite Trader
- Subtitle: Trusted Cross-Border TCG Exchange + Experience Layer
- One-liner: Authenticate, price fairly, trade safely, play together.

## 2. Market Pain (Who We Serve)

- Retail collectors: high grading cost, fake/repack risk, low online trust.
- Dealers/shops: high CAC, fixed cost pressure, regional growth limits.
- Players/community: fragmented events and weak online-offline play loop.

## 3. Why Now

- TCG value and participation are rapidly globalizing.
- Fraud risk and pricing inefficiency are increasing faster than trust infrastructure.
- Existing channels optimize transactions, not trust and retention flywheels.

## 4. Product Thesis

- One platform, four pillars:
  - Trust infrastructure
  - Fair liquidity
  - Merchant efficiency
  - Community growth
- Every claim in the pitch is backed by a visible, clickable demo interaction.

## 5. Demo Accounts + Interactive Flow

- Account picker at entry:
  - Collector demo account
  - Dealer demo account
  - RSA Inspector demo account
- RSA inspector flow:
  - Login
  - Accept mission
  - Scan card
  - Compare serial/fingerprint
  - Submit verdict
  - Issue verification badge

## 6. Interactive Modules (Button-Driven)

- Cross-border logistics tracking:
  - Route selector
  - Custody checkpoints
  - Timeline progression
- Unified HK TCG streaming hub:
  - Channel tabs
  - Room cards
  - Join-room interaction
- Tournament brackets + team league APIs:
  - Format toggle
  - Bracket preview
  - API payload simulation

## 7. Trust Proof

- RSA verification badges and risk flags.
- AI pre-grading to reduce wasted grading spend.
- Settlement-protection states visible in marketplace flow.
- KPI proof in UI: verification pass rate and settlement speed.

## 8. Liquidity + Merchant Value

- Fair-price indicators and verified sale references.
- Cross-border logistics visibility reduces transaction friction.
- Merchant value: lower CAC through platform demand and repeat listing behavior.

## 9. Community Growth Loop

- Streaming hub centralizes HK TCG discovery and engagement.
- Brackets/team league increase recurring activity.
- Engagement converts into listings, bids, and repeat transactions.

## 10. Go-To-Market

- Start with HK as a trust-first wedge market.
- Onboard trusted dealers and active communities first.
- Use events and league content to build repeat traffic.

## 11. KPI Scoreboard (First Two Quarters)

- Verification completion rate
- GMV under protected settlement flow
- Seller repeat listing rate
- Event participation -> transaction conversion
- Streaming session -> marketplace action conversion

## 12. Ask

- Strategic distribution partners and merchant onboarding pipeline
- Pilot events and regional ecosystem collaboration
- Support for cross-border expansion and integration partners
